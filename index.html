About me

My name is Stella Ishida, and I am a nutritionist dedicated to helping people achieve better health, balance, and performance through evidence-based nutrition. I graduated from Harvard University, where I built a strong academic background in clinical nutrition, human metabolism, and performance science.

I specialize in working with individuals who face eating disorders, providing personalized nutritional guidance focused on recovery, emotional well-being, and the development of a healthy relationship with food. I believe that nutrition goes beyond physical health and plays an essential role in mental and emotional balance.

I also work closely with high-performance athletes, creating strategic nutrition plans designed to improve endurance, recovery, strength, and overall athletic performance. My approach combines scientific precision with individualized care, ensuring that each patient receives support tailored to their unique needs and goals.

Services

Specially designed and personalized to help achieve your desired goals.

Eating disorder recovery treatment

treatment with nutritional support focuses on rebuilding a healthy relationship with food while improving both physical and emotional well-being. The process includes individualized meal planning, nutritional education, regular monitoring, and continuous support tailored to each patient’s needs and recovery goals. The treatment also works alongside mental health care to encourage balanced eating habits, restore nutritional health, and promote long-term recovery in a safe and compassionate environment.

Nutritional support for high-performance athletes

focuses on improving performance, recovery, body composition, and overall health through personalized nutrition strategies. The follow-up includes individualized meal plans, hydration guidance, supplementation when necessary, and continuous monitoring based on training intensity and competition goals. The process is designed to optimize energy levels, prevent injuries, enhance recovery, and support athletes in achieving their maximum performance safely and sustainably.

Weight loss treatment

focuses on creating healthy and sustainable lifestyle changes through personalized nutritional guidance and continuous support. The program includes individualized meal plans designed according to each patient’s goals, routine, and preferences, promoting balanced nutrition and long-term results. In addition, patients receive exclusive discounts on personalized training programs with a professional trainer partnered with the clinic, allowing nutrition and exercise to work together for more effective and lasting outcomes.

Muscle gain nutritional support

designed to help patients increase muscle mass in a healthy and efficient way through personalized nutrition and continuous monitoring. The treatment includes individualized meal plans tailored to each person’s body composition, routine, and training goals, ensuring the proper intake of nutrients to optimize muscle growth, recovery, and performance. Patients also receive exclusive discounts on personalized workout programs with a professional trainer partnered with the clinic, creating a complete approach that combines nutrition and training for the best possible results.
